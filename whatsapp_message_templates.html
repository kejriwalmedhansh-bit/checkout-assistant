Pacing, not just wording

Before any reply that takes real time, WhatsApp's native typing… indicator shows — and re-fires every ~20s for as long as the wait continues, since Meta's own indicator otherwise expires after 25s.

Once a reply is ready, messages go out 2 seconds apart rather than in one burst, so a fast response still reads as a paced conversation instead of five bubbles landing at once.

Fallback messages

Static, no placeholders. Each fires from a different edge case.

Non-text message type, or unparseable text from a returning user.

Zero search results, or no trusted candidate survived filtering.
